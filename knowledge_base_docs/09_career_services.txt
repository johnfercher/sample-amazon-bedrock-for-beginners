Greenfield University — Career Services FAQ

Q: Where is the Career Services office?
A: Career Services is located in the Student Center, Suite 210. Walk-in hours are Monday through Friday, 1:00 to 4:00 PM. Appointments can be scheduled online at greenfield.edu/careers.

Q: Can Career Services help me find an internship?
A: Yes. Career Services maintains a job and internship board on Handshake. Advisors can help with resume reviews, mock interviews, and internship search strategies. We also host an Internship Fair each October.

Q: When is the Career Fair?
A: The Fall Career Fair is held the last Wednesday of September. The Spring Career Fair is the second Wednesday of February. Both take place in the Field House from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Q: Does Greenfield offer resume and cover letter reviews?
A: Yes. Drop off your resume at the Career Services front desk for a written review within 48 hours, or schedule a one-on-one appointment for real-time feedback.

Q: Are there on-campus recruiting events?
A: Yes. Employers visit campus throughout the year for information sessions and interviews. A schedule is posted on Handshake and updated weekly.

Q: Can alumni use Career Services?
A: Greenfield alumni have lifetime access to Career Services, including the job board, career advising, and networking events.
