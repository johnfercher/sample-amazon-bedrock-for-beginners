Greenfield University — Dining Services FAQ

Q: What meal plan options are available?
A: Greenfield offers three meal plans: the Unlimited Plan (unlimited swipes, $2,800/semester), the 14-Meal Plan (14 swipes per week, $2,400/semester), and the 10-Meal Plan (10 swipes per week, $2,000/semester). All plans include $150 in Flex Dollars.

Q: Are first-year students required to have a meal plan?
A: Yes. All first-year students living on campus must select either the Unlimited Plan or the 14-Meal Plan.

Q: Where can I eat on campus?
A: The main dining hall is Harvest Commons in the Student Center, open 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Additional options include the Byte Cafe in the Turing Building, the Green Bean coffee shop in the Library, and Fireside Grill near the athletic complex.

Q: Can I use Flex Dollars off campus?
A: No. Flex Dollars are accepted only at on-campus dining locations. Unused Flex Dollars roll over between fall and spring semesters but expire at the end of the academic year.

Q: Are there options for dietary restrictions?
A: Yes. Harvest Commons has dedicated stations for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal meals. Allergen information is posted at each station and available on the Greenfield Dining app.

Q: Can guests eat in the dining hall?
A: Yes. Guest meals can be purchased at the door for $12 each, or students can use a guest swipe (each plan includes 3 guest swipes per semester).
