Greenfield University — Academic Calendar FAQ

Q: When does the Fall 2026 semester start?
A: The Fall 2026 semester begins on Monday, August 24, 2026. Move-in for residential students starts August 21.

Q: What is the add/drop deadline for Fall 2026?
A: The last day to add or drop a course without a "W" on your transcript is Friday, September 4, 2026. After this date, dropped courses will appear as a withdrawal.

Q: When is the last day to withdraw from a course?
A: The withdrawal deadline for Fall 2026 is Friday, October 30, 2026. A grade of "W" will appear on your transcript.

Q: When is Spring Break?
A: Spring Break for the 2026-2027 academic year is March 8 through March 12, 2027.

Q: When are final exams?
A: Fall 2026 final exams run from December 7 through December 11, 2026. The final exam schedule is posted on the Registrar's website by mid-October.

Q: Is there a winter session?
A: Yes. Greenfield offers a 3-week Winter Session from January 4 to January 22, 2027. Students may take one course during this period. Registration opens November 1.
